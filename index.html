Jorge Medina Pajares

Program Manager at Mirada PLC

Telecommunications | Video OTT | PayTV | Software Development

Jorge Medina is a Manager with more than 15 years of experience in sectors like Telco, PayTV/OTT Video Services and IoT. Jorge is a graduate of the Pontificia Universidad Católica (PUCP) in Lima - Perú, where he studied Industrial Engineering and an Executive MBA graduate of IE Business School in Madrid - Spain.

After graduating from engineering school, Jorge started working for Telefónica, one of the World's Top 10 Telecommunications Companies, where he assumed roles in Product and Project Management in innovative projects like Connected Home (IoT) and OTT Video services within Telefonica Digital, a división focussed on advanced digital services. In the recent years Jorge has assumed the role of Program Manager to deliver next-gen PayTV and video streaming solutions for multiple telcos in Europe and Latam.

Jorge is known for his extensive experience in project management with focus in agile methodologies, business opportunity assessment, value proposition design and new products definition.

When he is not working in digital projects, Jorge enjoys singing and playing the electric guitar in a rock band.
